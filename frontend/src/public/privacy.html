Privacy Policy

Daybook · by Torama Technologies

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how Torama Technologies ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Daybook application and website at daybook.torama.money (the "Service"). Daybook is a business operations tool used by companies to record daily sales, expenses, staff activity, inventory, and reports.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google to identify you and create your account.
  • Business and operational data. Information you and your colleagues enter into the Service — for example sales, payments, expenses, vendors, customers, staff records, attendance, inventory, cash records, reports, receipts, and notes.
  • Uploads. Files you attach, such as transfer receipts, staff photos, and documents.
  • Usage and device data. Basic technical information needed to operate the Service, such as log entries, the actions you take in the app (audit log), and your role and site assignment.

2. How we use information

  • To provide, operate, and secure the Service and your company's workspace.
  • To show you the data and features appropriate to your role and site.
  • To send operational emails you or your company configure, such as daily reports, invitations, and notifications to administrators.
  • To maintain records, prevent misuse, and troubleshoot problems.

3. How information is shared

Your company's data is visible to authorised members of that company according to their role. We do not sell your personal information or your company's data. We share information only:

  • Within your company, to members who are entitled to see it;
  • With service providers that host or deliver the Service on our behalf (for example our hosting provider and the email relay used to send messages);
  • Where required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users and the Service.

4. Storage and security

Data is stored on secure servers operated for Torama Technologies. We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it, including access controls by role and site, encrypted transport (HTTPS), and audit logging. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data retention

We keep company data for as long as the company uses the Service or as needed to provide it. When a member leaves a company, their access is disabled but the records they created are retained as part of the company's business history. Companies may request export or deletion of their data, subject to legal and record-keeping requirements.

6. Your choices and rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. Administrators of a company control member access and most company data. Some records must be retained for the company's accounting and audit purposes.

7. Children

The Service is intended for business use by adults and is not directed to children.

8. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above.

9. Contact

Questions about this Policy or your data: support@torama.money.
